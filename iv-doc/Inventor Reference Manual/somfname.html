Names are short series of characters generally used for labels or names, and are stored in a special tabledesigned to allow fast lookup and comparison. For most purposes, an SoMFString field is probably more appropriate. SoMFNames are written to file as one or more strings of characters. Names mustbegin with an underscore or alphabetic character, and must consist entirely of underscores, alphabetic characters, or numbers.
