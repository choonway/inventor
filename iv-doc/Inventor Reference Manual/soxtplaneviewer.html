SoXtPlaneViewer
NAME
SoXtPlaneViewer -- viewer component which moves the camera in a plane
INHERITS FROM
SoXtComponent > SoXtGLWidget > SoXtRenderArea > SoXtViewer > SoXtFullViewer > SoXtPlaneViewer
DESCRIPTION
The Plane viewer component allows the user to translate the camera in the viewing plane, as well as dolly (move foward/backward) and zoom in and out. The vieweralso allows the user to roll the camera (rotate around the forward direction) and seek to objects which will specify a new viewing plane. This viewer could be used formodeling, in drafting, and architectural work. The camera can be aligned to the X, Y or Z axes.

USAGE
Left Mouse: Translate up, down, left and right. Ctrl + Left Mouse: Used for roll action (rotates around the viewer forward direction). <s> + Left Mouse: Alternative to the Seek button. Press (but do not hold down) the<s> key, then click on a target object. Middle Mouse: Dolly in and out (gets closer to and further away from the object). Right Mouse: Open the popup menu.
